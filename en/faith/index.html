i know we weren't the closest when you died and i regret that everyday

i wish that you could have seen the people around you grow old and that it didn't have to end like this

i wish that we could have been faster and i wish that i could've helped

i guess that there's no point trying to worry myself with what could and what couldn't have happened

you showed me so many things which i still cling to today and i sometimes wonder what my life, and the lives

of others around you would have been without you.
